Cryptocoin Ticker Images

This is a simple app based on the idea of the Bitcoin Tickers app. Given a special URL, the app will generate images representing the value of a particular coin.

For example, to generate an image for the current highest bid price for DVC in BTC on Vircurex, use the following URL:
http://dvcticker.appspot.com/vircurex/dvc/btc.png
The resulting image:

To see how much 100.00BTC is worth in USD:
http://dvcticker.appspot.com/vircurex/100.00btc/usd.png
The resulting image:

Supported exchanges:
Vircurex. Supported currencies are:
BTC, USD, EUR, DVC, IXC, LTC, NMC, PPC, TRC, FTC.
MTGox. Supported currencies (one must be BTC):
BTC, USD, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, DKK, EUR, GBP, HKD, JPY, NZD, PLN, RUB, SEK, SGD, THB.
BTC-E. Supported currency pairs (can be forward or reverse):
BTC/USD, BTC/RUR, BTC/EUR, LTC/BTC, LTC/USD, LTC/RUR, NMC/BTC, USD/RUR, EUR/USD, NVC/BTC, TRC/BTC, PPC/BTC, FTC/BTC

All images are based on the highest bid price in the forward direction and lowest ask price in the reverse.)
